It all starts with our great Menu!

Welcome to the Brunswick Barbecue & Brew, your destination for the best in barbecue! It didn't take long for us to earn our reputation as one of the most popular eateries in the Capital Region. Noted for a relaxed and comfortable atmosphere, Brunswick Barbecue & Brew is the perfect place to unwind. Pick from our great beer selection, and let one of our courteous waitstaff answer the question you're bound to ask... "What is that great smell coming from the kitchen?"

Owned and operated by Master Brewer Gary "Goose" Gosselin, Brunswick Barbecue & Brew is the culmination of his dream to create the perfect blend of great beers, great barbecue, and live music all under one big, red roof. Sampling some of the finest ribs you will ever taste is just the beginning, as there are a wealth of things to enjoy at BB&B. Great weekend entertainment, a superior beer selection overseen by the brewmaster himself, full bar featuring creative cocktails, and an extensive wine list are just a few of the things that will keep you coming back for more!

Finding just the right dish to satisfy your craving is never a problem. BB&B offers 4 different menus to suit every taste. In addition to unique and savory barbecue selections, you can enjoy seafood, steak and lighter fare as well. Keep in mind we also do catering offsite along with private parties!

Located at the intersection of Routes 2 and 351 in Brunswick, NY. The restaurant is easy to get to, just minutes east of Troy. We are open 7 days a week at 5 PM. For reservations, call 518-279-9993. Hope to see you soon!
