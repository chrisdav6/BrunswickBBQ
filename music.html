Live Music at Brunswick Barbecue & Brew

Throughout the year you can enjoy the best in live, local music at Brunswick Barbecue & Brew. During the summer months on Saturday nights you can hear the area's finest local bands outside on our patio. On Friday nights we feature the finest in eclectic and entertaining performers all year round.
